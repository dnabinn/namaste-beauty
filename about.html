About Us

Namaste Beauty Studio

Beauty, Care &
Dedication

Namaste Beauty Studio was born from a genuine passion for beauty and a deep belief that every person deserves to feel their absolute best. We created a space where you can relax, be pampered, and leave feeling transformed.

Located in the vibrant heart of Lisboa, on Rua Passos Manuel, our studio offers a complete range of professional beauty services — from hair and nails to facials, waxing, lash extensions, and threading.

Every treatment is performed by skilled, dedicated professionals who care not just about results, but about the entire experience you have with us. From the moment you walk in, you are our priority.
